# Testimonials

Here you can **share your retreat experiences**: write a testimonial, choose who can see it and, if you want, offer it for the site's public page.

## Writing a testimonial

Tap **New testimonial** and fill in the form:

- **Your testimonial**: the text of your experience (between 10 and 2000 characters).
- **Retreat (optional)**: you can link it to a specific retreat or leave it without one.

## Visibility

You choose who can see your testimonial:

- **Private**: only you.
- **Retreat participants**: those who took part in the same retreat.
- **Connected brothers**: only your connected brothers.
- **Public**: any user on the platform.

## Public page (landing)

If you check **Allow publication on the landing page**, your testimonial could appear on the site's public page, always **subject to administrator approval**.

## Managing your testimonials

In the list you can **edit** or **delete** the testimonials you published. Administrators can additionally approve or revoke a testimonial's appearance on the landing page.
