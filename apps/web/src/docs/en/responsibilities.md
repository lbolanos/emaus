# Responsibilities and Service Teams

📺 **[Watch the tutorial](https://youtu.be/3XZlQJZlcmo)** (Spanish)

These two screens organize the retreat's service: **Responsibilities** assigns tasks and talks to individual servers; **Service Teams** groups servers into teams (kitchen, music, palancas, logistics, etc.).

## Responsibilities

Each card is a responsibility or talk. You can:

- **Create a responsibility** and edit or delete them.
- **Assign** a server to a responsibility, or **assign a speaker** to a talk (searching the system or entering them manually); remove the assignment with the X.
- View **documentation** and download support files (dynamics), open per-responsibility **attachments**, and, when a related team exists, **add servers to the team**.
- Filter by **All / Talks / Responsibilities / Unassigned**, search, change the number of columns, **export to Word**, and **print**.

## Service Teams

Each team has a type, a leader, and its members. You can:

- **Add a team** (choose the type and name) or **Create default teams** if none exist yet; delete them with confirmation.
- **Drag servers** between the "without team" lists and the team cards to assign or remove them.
- Search by team or by server, view each team's **instructions**, adjust columns, **export to Word**, and **print**.
