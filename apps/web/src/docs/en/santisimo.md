# Chapel Watch Schedule

Here you organize **adoration of the Blessed Sacrament** by the hour during the retreat. You generate time slots between two dates, enable a public link so the community ("angelitos") can sign up, and manage sign-ups from the three-column calendar (one day per column).

## Generate the slots

Click **Generate**: pick a start and end date/time, the **length** of each slot (e.g. 60 min) and the **capacity** (people per slot). The default runs from Friday afternoon to Sunday morning. Check **Clear existing** to start from scratch.

## Manage each slot

For each slot you can:

- **Add name**: sign up a server (by searching) or an angelito (name only; phone and email optional).
- **Not required**: disable the hour without deleting the slot; it shows greyed out.
- **Delete** the slot or remove a sign-up with the ✕.

## Public link and more actions

Turn on the **Enable** checkbox and, if the retreat is public, use **⋮ More actions → Copy public link** to share the page where the community signs up themselves. That menu also lets you **Print**, **Auto-assign angelitos** to meal hours, and **Delete all and regenerate** from the Minute-by-Minute schedule (destructive).
