# Minute by Minute

The **live retreat agenda**, split by day. Each item is an activity with its time, duration, owner and script. It replaces the paper "minute by minute" sheet: the coordinator drives it here in real time and servers see changes instantly on their devices.

📺 **[Watch the tutorial (2 min)](https://youtu.be/YYwjzHcumpA)**

## During the retreat (per-row controls)

- **▶ Start**: marks the item as "in progress". Notifies the owner and shows as NOW on the public screen.
- **✓ Complete**: closes the active item. Only appears while the item is in progress.
- **−5 / +5**: shifts the item (and the rest of the day) 5 minutes back or forward.
- **📎 Documents**: opens the role scripts (editable markdown, PDF, images). The number is the count.
- **Click the row**: opens the full modal (time, duration, owner, notes, status, actual times).

## Reordering items (drag & drop)

Only when grouping **by Day**: drag one row onto another of the same day to swap their positions. Times are preserved (each slot keeps its original time); you change **which** item occupies each slot.

## "More actions" menu (⋮)

- **🔔 Ring bell**: fires an on-screen notification to all connected servers.
- **🖨 Print**: print view of the whole schedule in A4.
- **📦 Download scripts (zip)**: bundle of all markdowns and PDFs, organized by responsibility folder. Useful offline.
- **📺 Copy public screen link**: login-less big-screen view at `/mam/<slug>` for projecting in the room. Reflects live what the coordinator marks as NOW.
- **⏱ Shift day**: moves all items of the day by N minutes. Useful when the whole retreat runs late.
- **✨ Auto-assign angelitos**: fills the Santísimo slots during meals with available "Angelito" participants.
- **📥 Import from template** (overwrite): deletes all current items and re-materializes from the template. **Careful: loses shifts and reorders.**

## Public screen

The `/mam/<slug>` link opens a login-less view meant for projecting. It shows the activity marked as NOW and the next one, updating in real time as the coordinator advances the agenda.
