# Houses

Here you manage the houses where retreats take place, along with their rooms and beds. Each house stores its name, address, and the list of beds later used to assign participants.

## Search and list

Use the search box to filter by **name** or **address**. The table shows each house with its address and the **number of beds** configured.

## Add and edit

- **Add House** opens the form to enter a new house's details.
- **Edit** modifies an existing house.
- **Delete** removes the house (it asks for confirmation).

## Bed map

The **Map** button (or the map icon next to the bed count) opens the house's bed map, where you define beds by **room** and **floor**, their **type** (normal, bunk, or mattress), and their default **usage** (walker or server). Changes are saved to the house.
