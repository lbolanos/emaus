# Food

This view lists walkers focused on their dietary needs, so the kitchen can prepare the right menus. It shows whether each participant **has dietary restrictions** and the **details** of those restrictions.

## View and search

The table shows the participant's number, name, whether they have restrictions, and the details. Use the **search box** to locate someone, and sort by tapping a column header.

## Edit the information

Tap the **edit** icon on a participant's row to open their form and update whether they have restrictions and the corresponding detail. First and last name are not editable here.

## Columns, import, and export

From the actions menu you can **choose columns**, **import** or **export** to Excel/CSV, and **print** the list.
