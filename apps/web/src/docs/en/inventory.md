# Inventory

Here you manage the **retreat supplies**: what's needed, how much you have, and what's left to buy. Each retreat starts from the global item catalog, and you adjust quantities, boxes, and statuses as logistics progresses.

## Quantities and recalculate

With **Recalculate** the system computes the required quantity of each item based on walkers (registered or expected) and the catalog ratio. You edit the **Actual** column yourself; the **To buy** column shows the shortfall in red. A banner at the top summarizes **insufficient items**.

## Adding and configuring items

- **Add item**: pick items from the global catalog, or create a **new one for this retreat only**.
- **Config for this retreat** (in each row's ⋮ menu): override the ratio, set a fixed quantity, or **exclude** the item, without touching the global catalog.
- Set each item's **cycle status**: Pending, Packed, On site, Consumed, or Returned. You can also assign a **Box** and notes.

## Filters, catalog, and more actions

Search by name, team, or box; group by category, team, box, or status; and toggle which **columns** you see. From **More actions**: copy inventory from a previous retreat, print the **packing list** or the **shopping list**, import/export Excel, and view the **change history**.

## Global catalog (Inventory Items)

Under **Settings → Inventory Items** you maintain the master catalog shared across all retreats: name, category, team, unit, **ratio per walker** or fixed quantity, and whether the item is active. Editing an item here affects every retreat that uses it.
