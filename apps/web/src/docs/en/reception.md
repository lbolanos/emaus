# Reception (arrival check-in)

Reception is the **check-in** board for the retreat day: you mark who has arrived, see how many are missing and follow progress in real time. It's designed to be used from your phone at the door.

## Progress panel

At the top you see three cards: the **Total** of participants, how many have **arrived** and how many are **pending**, plus a progress bar with the percentage. On the retreat day, if the retreat has an arrival time set, the **expected arrival time** appears along with an indicator of how long is left or how much time has passed.

## Marking arrivals

In the **pending** list, tap **Arrived** next to a person to record their check-in; their number, phone and amount paid show next to the name. The list of **those who already arrived** is collapsible and shows the check-in time; there you can **undo** an arrival if you made a mistake.

## Search and sort

- **Search** by first name, last name or participant number (search is available in both pending and arrived).
- **Sort** by last name, first name or number; your preference is remembered.

## Real time

The board updates on its own when someone else marks an arrival or assembles a bag, so several people can receive at once without reloading the page.
