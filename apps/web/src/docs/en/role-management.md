# Retreat Role Management

Here you control **who has access to your retreat and what they can do**. When someone creates an
Emaús account they don't see any retreat yet — you grant them access from this screen first.

📺 **[Watch the tutorial](https://youtu.be/-5nbC10cPQg)** · It's in the menu, under **Administration → Role Management**.

## Role requests

If someone requests access to your retreat, their request shows under **"Role Requests"**, where
you can **approve or reject** it.

## Granting access

Two ways:

- **Invite User** — enter one or more **emails** and pick a role. Best for people not yet on the
  platform; they receive an email invitation.
- **Quick Assign** — **search by name** for someone who **already uses Emaús** and assign the role
  instantly, without typing emails. Available only to the retreat creator.

## What each role can do

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| **Administrator** | The whole retreat: participants, houses, tables, payments, inventory, schedule, tasks, templates — and **user & role management**. |
| **Treasurer** | Money-focused: **payments** and participants (incl. scholarship amount). Everything else read-only. |
| **Logistics** | Inventory, tables, houses, responsibilities, participants, payments and the schedule. No user management. |
| **Communications** | Message templates and sending; read access to participants, payments and tables. |
| **Server** | Basic read-only: view participants, retreat, schedule, tasks and shirts. No editing. |

Each user in the retreat shows their **role** and **status** (active or pending acceptance).
