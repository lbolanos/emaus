# Social Features

The social features allow you to connect with other users in the Emaus community. You can manage your profile, add friends, follow users, and search for people with shared interests.

## Profile Management

### Your Profile

Your profile is your public presence in the Emaus community. It includes:

- **Avatar**: Your profile picture
- **Bio**: A short description about yourself
- **Location**: Where you're based
- **Website**: Link to your website or social media
- **Interests**: Topics you're passionate about
- **Skills**: Abilities or expertise you can share
- **Privacy Settings**: Control what information is visible to others

### Editing Your Profile

1. Navigate to **Profile** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Edit** button
3. Update your information
4. Click **Save** to confirm

### Uploading an Avatar

1. Navigate to **Profile**
2. Click on your current avatar or the upload icon
3. Select an image from your device
4. The image will be automatically resized and optimized

> **Tip**: Use a square image for best results. The system will automatically resize large images.

### Removing Your Avatar

1. Navigate to **Profile**
2. Click the **Remove** button on your avatar
3. Confirm the removal

## Finding Users

### Searching for Users

1. Navigate to **Search Users** in the sidebar
2. Enter a name or email in the search box
3. Use filters to narrow results by:
   - **Interests**: Users with specific interests
   - **Skills**: Users with particular skills
   - **Location**: Users in a specific area
   - **Retreat**: Users from a specific retreat

4. Click on a user card to view their public profile

### Viewing Public Profiles

Public profiles show information that users have chosen to share, including:

- Display name
- Bio
- Location
- Interests and skills
- Avatar

## Friends

### Adding Friends

1. Search for the user you want to add
2. Visit their public profile
3. Click **Send Friend Request**
4. The user will receive a notification and can accept or decline

### Managing Friend Requests

**Viewing Pending Requests:**

1. Navigate to **Friends**
2. Click on **Pending** to see received requests
3. Click **Accept** or **Reject** on each request

**Viewing Sent Requests:**

1. Navigate to **Friends**
2. Click on **Sent** to see your pending requests

### Viewing Friends

1. Navigate to **Friends**
2. Your friend list shows all accepted connections
3. Click on a friend to view their profile

### Removing a Friend

1. Navigate to **Friends**
2. Find the friend you want to remove
3. Click **Remove** next to their name
4. Confirm the removal

> **Note**: Removing a friend will remove the connection for both users.

## Following

Followers allow you to stay connected with users without being friends. This is useful for keeping up with people you admire or work with.

### Following a User

1. Search for the user or visit their profile
2. Click **Follow**
3. You'll now see their updates in your activity feed

### Viewing Your Followers

1. Navigate to **Followers**
2. See all users who follow you

### Viewing Who You Follow

1. Navigate to **Following**
2. See all users you're following

### Unfollowing a User

1. Navigate to **Following**
2. Find the user you want to unfollow
3. Click **Unfollow**

## Blocking

Blocking prevents another user from interacting with you.

### Blocking a User

1. Visit the user's profile
2. Click **Block**
3. Confirm the block

Blocked users cannot:

- Send you friend requests
- Follow you
- See your profile or activity

### Viewing Blocked Users

1. Navigate to your profile settings
2. View your blocked list
3. Click **Unblock** to remove a block

## Privacy Settings

Control what information is visible to others:

- **Show Email**: Allow others to see your email address
- **Show Phone**: Allow others to see your phone number
- **Show Retreats**: Allow others to see which retreats you've attended

> **Security Tip**: Only share contact information with people you trust.

## Tips for Using Social Features

1. **Complete Your Profile**: A complete profile helps others find and connect with you
2. **Add Interests and Skills**: This helps users with similar interests find you
3. **Be Respectful**: Follow community guidelines when interacting with others
4. **Review Privacy Settings**: Regularly check what information you're sharing
5. **Report Issues**: If someone is behaving inappropriately, contact an administrator
