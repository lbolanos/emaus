# Participant Status: Waiting List, Canceled and Notes

These three screens help you track participants by their situation in the retreat: who is on the waiting list, who canceled, and where to record cancellations and per-person notes.

## Waiting List

Shows participants marked as **waiting** (when the retreat capacity is exceeded) who do **not yet have a table assigned**. From here you see their number, name, type (walker or server) and whether they are canceled, so you can decide who to promote when a spot opens up.

## Canceled

Lists the retreat's **canceled** participants. You'll see name, email, phone, parish, remaining balance and type, to follow up (for example, refunds or contact). The email is not editable; the other fields are.

## Cancellation and Notes

This is the screen to **mark or unmark a participant's cancellation** and capture **notes** about their case (reason, agreements, reminders). You edit the canceled checkbox and the notes field directly in the table or in the participant form.

## How it works

- **Canceling** does not delete the participant: it only marks them as canceled (their data is kept).
- Once canceled, the person no longer counts in the active lists and appears under **Canceled**.
- Use **Cancellation and Notes** to reverse a cancellation or to record the reason.
