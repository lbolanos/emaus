# My Schedule

This screen shows **what's assigned to you** during the retreat: only the Minute-by-Minute activities where you are listed as a lead or support. It's each server's personal view, not the retreat's full schedule.

## Upcoming and past

- **Upcoming**: activities that haven't ended yet, sorted by time. The one in progress is highlighted in green.
- **Past**: those already finished or marked completed, shown dimmed.

Each card shows the time, the name, your **role** (🎤), the location (📍), notes, and any downloadable attachments.

## Real-time notifications

While you're on this screen you get an alert **10, 5, and 0 minutes before** each assigned activity, and when one starts. The indicator at the top tells you whether you're **connected (WS)** in real time.

## If you see nothing

If a message says your account has no linked participant, ask the **coordinator** to link you in this retreat; without that link the schedule can't show your assignments.
