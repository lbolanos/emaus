# Managing Servers

This section explains how to manage servers (servidores) - team members who serve during the retreat.

## Overview

Servers are the volunteers and team members who help facilitate the retreat. They may have different roles and responsibilities:

- **Leaders (Líderes)**: Table leaders
- **Co-leaders (Colíderes)**: Assist table leaders
- **Service Team**: Various support roles

## Adding a Server

To add a new server:

1. Navigate to **Servers** in the sidebar
2. Click the **Add Server** button
3. Fill in the required information:
   - Name and contact details
   - Role/position
   - Skills or experience
4. Click **Save**

## Server Roles

Different server roles have different responsibilities:

- **Table Leader**: Leads a small group of walkers
- **Co-leader**: Assists the table leader
- **Music**: Leads music and worship
- **Kitchen**: Manages food service
- **Logistics**: Handles materials and supplies

## Assigning Roles

1. Open the server's details
2. Select their role from the dropdown
3. Assign them to a specific table if applicable
4. Save the assignment

## Importing Servers

Similar to walkers, servers can be imported from an Excel file:

1. Click **Import**
2. Select your Excel file
3. Map columns to system fields
4. Review and confirm

## Server Information

Track important information for each server:

- Past experience with retreats
- Special skills or certifications
- Availability dates
- Notes about their service
