# Notes and Meeting Points

This view lists walkers focused on how they arrive at the retreat: their **meeting point / pickup location**, whether they **arrive on their own**, and each person's **notes**. It's useful for organizing transport and arrival logistics.

## View and search

The table shows the participant's number, name, notes, pickup location, and whether they arrive on their own. Use the **search box** to find someone, and sort by tapping a column header.

## Edit the information

Tap the **edit** icon on a participant's row to open their form and update their meeting point, the "arrives on own" option, and their notes. First and last name are not editable here.

## Columns, import, and export

From the actions menu you can **choose columns**, **import** or **export** to Excel/CSV, and **print** the list.
