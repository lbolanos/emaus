# Preparations

📺 **[Watch the video tutorial](https://youtu.be/Zt6tmd1A2O0)**

**Preparations** are the weekly meetings to get the **servant team** ready before the retreat. Each meeting works through a talk with a specific objective (Service, Knowing Yourself, Healing and Forgiveness, Family and Friends, Word and Prayer, Trust, The Father's Love) so the team arrives prepared to give them. They are not material for the walkers: they are the formation of the team that will serve the retreat.

## The calendar

- When you create a retreat the calendar is generated automatically: **seven weekly meetings** ending just before the retreat, each with its talk document attached.
- **Set up and create**: choose how many weeks (7 to 9 is typical), the day and the time. The resulting dates are shown before you create. Keep **“Attach default documents”** checked to include each talk's material.
- Every meeting is editable in place: change its title, date and time directly. Use **Add entry** to add a standalone meeting.

## Documents for each talk

- **Upload file**: attach a PDF or Word file with the talk guide.
- **New text**: write a text (markdown) document editable inside the app, without uploading files.
- The team views and downloads them from the public link, no account needed.

## Skipping a date for a holiday

If a meeting falls on a holiday, click **Skip for holiday** and note the reason. That date is marked as a holiday and **the earlier meetings move one week earlier**: the first one starts sooner. The retreat date never moves.

## Public link for the team

**Copy public link** gives you an address to share over WhatsApp. Servants open it with no username or password: they see the calendar, the **highlighted next meeting**, and download each talk's document. It requires the retreat to have a link (slug) and be marked as public.
