# Payment Tracking

This section explains how to track and manage payments for retreat participants.

## Overview

The payment tracking system helps you manage:

- Registration payments
- Scholarship awards
- Payment status tracking
- Payment history
- Outstanding balances

## Payment Status

Each participant can have one of these payment statuses:

- **Pending**: No payment received yet
- **Partial**: Partial payment received
- **Paid in Full**: Complete payment received
- **Scholarship**: Participant received a scholarship
- **Scholarship Partial**: Scholarship + partial payment
- **Overdue**: Payment is past due

## Recording a Payment

To record a payment for a participant:

1. Navigate to **Payments** in the sidebar
2. Find the participant
3. Click **Add Payment**
4. Enter payment details:
   - Amount
   - Payment method (cash, check, card, transfer)
   - Payment date
   - Reference number (if applicable)
5. Click **Save**

## Managing Scholarships

### Awarding a Scholarship

1. Open the participant's payment details
2. Click **Add Scholarship**
3. Enter scholarship amount
4. Select scholarship fund source
5. Add notes about the scholarship decision
6. Click **Save**

### Scholarship Funds

Track scholarship funds separately:

- Total available scholarship budget
- Amount awarded to date
- Remaining scholarship funds

## Payment Reports

Generate various payment reports:

### Payment Status Report

- Shows all participants with their payment status
- Filter by status (pending, paid, overdue, etc.)
- Export to Excel or PDF

### Outstanding Balance Report

- Participants with unpaid balances
- Contact information for follow-up
- Amount owed per participant

### Scholarship Report

- Total scholarships awarded
- Breakdown by fund source
- Recipient list (confidential)

## Importing Payments

You can import payment records:

1. Click **Import Payments**
2. Select Excel file with payment data
3. Map columns to payment fields
4. Review and confirm import

## Payment Reminders

Track follow-up actions:

- Send reminders to pending payments
- Mark reminder dates
- Track response to payment requests

## Financial Reports

Access financial summaries:

- Total collected vs. expected
- Payment method breakdown
- Scholarship impact on budget
- Cash flow projection
