# User Type and Table

This view lists all participants (walkers and servers) with an overview of their place in the retreat: their **table**, their **room**, the **parish**, who **invited** them, and their **type** (walker or server).

## View and search

The table shows the participant's number, name, table, room, parish, invited by, and type. Use the **search box** to find someone, and sort by tapping a column header.

## Edit the information

Tap the **edit** icon on a participant's row to open their form and adjust the editable fields (table, room, parish, invited by, type). First and last name are not editable here.

## Columns, import, and export

From the actions menu you can **choose columns**, **import** or **export** to Excel/CSV, and **print** the list.
