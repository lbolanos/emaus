# Retreat Dashboard

The Dashboard is the home screen for each retreat: it gathers the most important numbers and progress at a glance, with cards you can open to jump straight to each section.

## Header and shortcuts

At the top you see the parish, start date, and how many days are left (or whether the retreat has already started). From there you can **Invite someone** (grant retreat access), **View the flyer**, and **refresh the data** with the reload button.

## Registration links

The **Registration Links** section shows the links for walkers and servers. For each one you can **copy**, **open**, or show its **QR code** to share it.

## Stat cards

Each card summarizes part of the retreat and is clickable to open its section:

- **Participants**: walkers, servers, waiting list, and partial servers, with their capacity.
- **Responsibilities**, **Tables**, **Beds** (assigned / unassigned / floors used), and **Palancas**.
- **Pre-Retreat Tasks**: done, overdue, due this week, and unassigned.
- During the retreat, **Minute by Minute**, **Reception**, **Blessed Sacrament + angelitos**, and **Bags Report** become active.

## Customize

The gear icon opens the **Customize** panel: show or hide sections and reorder them. Each card can also be collapsed by tapping its title.
