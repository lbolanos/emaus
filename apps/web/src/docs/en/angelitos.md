# Angelitos

**Angelitos** are partial servers who help during the retreat, especially covering Blessed Sacrament guard shifts. This screen gathers their list and the control of their **time availability**.

## Availability status

At the top there's a table with each angelito and how many availability **blocks** they have configured. Those without a schedule yet are marked **"No schedule"**, and a summary shows how many of them are already configured.

## Edit availability and shifts

Tap **Configure** (or **Edit**) next to an angelito to open the editor. It has two tabs:

- **Availability**: you add the time blocks in which the person can serve (bounded by the retreat dates) and save them.
- **Assigned shifts**: shows the shifts already assigned to them, with their time and whether they are a meal window or an automatic assignment.
