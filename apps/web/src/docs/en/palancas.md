# Palancas

📺 **[Watch the tutorial](https://youtu.be/5mUo2Q1v3Ws)** (Spanish)

**Palancas** are the letters and support messages that walkers receive during the retreat. This screen helps you track how many were requested, how many arrived, and who coordinates them for each walker.

## What the table shows

For each walker you see their number, name and reference data such as the **last payment date** and the **message count**. These fields are read-only and serve as context.

## What you can record

The editable fields per walker are:

- **Palancas coordinator**: who is in charge of gathering their palancas.
- **Palancas requested**: how many were requested from family and friends.
- **Palancas received**: how many have arrived.
- **Palancas notes**: free-form remarks (pending items, agreements, reminders).

## How to use it

Edit the data directly in the table or by opening the walker's form, and compare **requested** against **received** to spot who is missing palancas and follow up before the retreat.
